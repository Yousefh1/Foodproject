Raspberries Smoothies

These ingredients are for one serving

  • 1 cup coconut milk (If you are allergic to it, use water milk that you desire
  • 1 1/2 cups Raspberries (Frozen)
  • 1 1/2 cups of spinach
  • 1 teaspoon Vanila extract
  • 1 serving of Protein Powder (Use any kind, I use the vanilla flavor)
  • 1 tbs coconut flakes

Directions

  • Blend spinach and coconut milk until smoothies
  • Add remaining ingredients, and blend until smooth
